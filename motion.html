    FAQ

    Frequently Asked Questions

    How many photos are required?

    For an effective 45-second cinematic promo, we recommend 10–18 professional listing photos.

    Is this AI or actual editing?

    This is human-guided cinematic editing. We do not use AI to hallucinate or change the property; we only add perspective motion to your real photos.

    What is the revision policy?

    Each order includes 1 minor revision (e.g. music swap or minor text correction) to ensure the final output is correct.

    What file format do I receive?

    You receive a high-resolution MP4 file perfectly formatted for realestate.com.au, YouTube, and all major social platforms.

    Can this be used for commercial listings?

    Yes. We produce cinematic promos for commercial, retail, and industrial properties where scale and professional motion are required.

    Is the video compatible with realestate.com.au?

    Yes. Every video is specifically optimized for direct upload to realestate.com.au, domain.com.au, and social media platforms.

    When does the 48-hour delivery start?

    Turnaround is measured from the moment we receive your final assets (complete photos, address, and instructions).
